About Me

My Senior High

Holy Child School, also known as Angel's Hill. The school motto is "Facta Non Verba" which simply means Actions Not Words. It is a female second-cycle institution in Cape Coast in the Central Region of Ghana. In 2003, the school was ranked among the best 10 schools in Africa

My First Degree

University of Cape Coast, Ghana. I obtained BSc Biochemistry. With over 200 programmes of study, modern facilities, and accomplished lecturers, its major goal is to prepare productive citizens and future leaders who can serve the national and global community in diverse ways.

My Second Degree

Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology. I obtained a Masters in Environmental Science. Its goal is to raise the next generation of ethical and transformational leaders who would influence change on the African Continent

My Place of Work

A rapidly growing, West African gold producer. Perseus operates two gold mines in West Africa and is developing its third mine. Gold production will increase to over 500,000 ounces per year in 2021/2022.
